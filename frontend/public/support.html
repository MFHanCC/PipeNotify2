Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why aren't my notifications appearing in Google Chat?
A: Check these common issues:
  • Verify your Google Chat webhook URL is correct and active
  • Ensure your notification rules are enabled
  • Run a live test from the Testing section in your dashboard
  • Check if the deal meets your rule criteria (value, stage, etc.)
Q: How do I get a Google Chat webhook URL?
A: Follow these steps:
  1. Open Google Chat in your browser
  2. Go to the Chat space where you want notifications
  3. Click the space name at the top
  4. Select "Manage webhooks"
  5. Click "Add webhook"
  6. Name it "Pipedrive Notifications" and copy the generated URL
Q: Can I send notifications to multiple Google Chat spaces?
A: Yes! You can add multiple webhook URLs and create different notification rules for each space. For example, send all deals to your sales team space and only high-value deals to your management space.
Q: How do I stop notifications during non-business hours?
A: Use the Quiet Hours feature in your settings. You can set your timezone, business hours, and choose whether to receive notifications on weekends.
Q: What Pipedrive events can trigger notifications?
A: PipeNotify supports all major Pipedrive events including:
  • Deal won/lost
  • Deal stage changes
  • New deals created
  • Deal updates (value, owner, etc.)
  • Contact and activity events
Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes. We use industry-standard security measures:
  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • OAuth 2.0 authentication with Pipedrive
  • Webhook URLs are stored securely
  • We only process data necessary for notifications
  • Regular security audits and monitoring

  4. Try Incognito Mode: This helps isolate browser-related problems
